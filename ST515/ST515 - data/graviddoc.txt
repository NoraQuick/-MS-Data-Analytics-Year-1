
GRAVID - numbers of embryos carried by gravid mysids in Lake Washington,
from samples taken in successive years (at roughly the same time of year).
Each record represents a gravid female.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

   cohort       year in which the mysid was collected (1 or 2)
   size         dorsal carapace length of mysid, in mm
   nembr        number of embryos in intact brood pouch
