
PHYTO - data from a ``meta-analysis'' of the effects of nutrients and 
fish on phytoplankton biomass in 11 studies.  A single replication of a 
nutrients x fish factorial design is available from each study.

	study	number of experiment (1-11)
	fish	0 = low fish, 1 = high fish
	nutr 	0 = low nutrients, 1 = high nutrients
	biomass	phytoplankton biomass
