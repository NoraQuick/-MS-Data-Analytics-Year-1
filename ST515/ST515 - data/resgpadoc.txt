
RESGPA - residency and GPA information for 90 students randomly selected
from a large population of OSU students that graduated in the 1990's.
The sampling was restricted to yield equal numbers of students (30) in
each residency group.
------------------------------------------------------------------------

RES	residency: 1 = OR resident, 2 = nonresident (out of state)
	           3 = international

CUMGPA	cumulative grade-point average at OSU (0 to 4)
