
BP0 - a small subset of the data from a clinical trial of an 
antihypertensive (blood-pressure-lowering) drug, for use in class
illustrations.  Each line represents a person who was randomly
assigned to one of three dose groups and treated for six weeks.
-----------------------------------------------------------------

DOSE	dose of the drug, in arbitrary units (0 = placebo)
CHANGE	change in diastolic blood pressure (final minus initial),
        in mm Hg
