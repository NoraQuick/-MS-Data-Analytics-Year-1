
CEP3 - sizes of macrozooplankton (cladocerans and copepods, excluding
nauplii) in six experimental ponds at Cornell University, three of which
contained planktivorous fish (bluegill sunfish and pumpkinseeds) and three
of which lacked fish.  Each record represents an individual zooplankter.

   pond         pond number (1 through 3); note that pond 1 in the no-fish
                   treatment is different from pond 1 in the fish treatment
   fish         0 = no fish, 1 = fish present
   time         1 = sample taken during the day, 2 = sample taken at night
   size         body size in mm
